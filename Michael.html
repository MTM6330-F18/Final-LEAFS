Meet the Professors

Michael Eisenbraun

eisenbm@algonquincollege.com

Michael is currently a professor at Algonquin College in the School of Media and Design. He teaches Client & Server-side Web Development for IMD. He is born and raised in South Dakota, USA, and immigrated to Canada in Aug. 2014. He is married with 3 children. He designed his very first web site in 1997. He graduated in 2005 from Iowa State University. And received first web design job in Nov. 2006. He started teaching in Jan. 2016. He teaches Client & Server-side Web Development.

1. Courses taught

I am teach Client-Side Web Development and Server-Side Web Development for the IMD program.

2. Background

I created my first web site in 1997 and continue to do web development as a hobby until my first web development job in 2006. I am largely self-taught and have receive very little formal training in web development. In 2016, after 10 years working professionally in the industry, I started teaching at Algonquin college.

3. Motivation in teaching

The field of web design and development is ever-changing and if you donâ€™t keep moving, you will fall behind. It is this realization that lead me to start teaching at Algonquin College in January of 2016. I have a passion for learning and it fuels my desire to share what I know with others. It has become my new passion, and my primary focus. I aim to be the best teacher I can, and it is my hope that my students will find the same spark of curiosity that changed my life all those years ago.

4. Unforgetable teaching experience

I once had a student who was struggling in my class to the point that she was going to fail. I offered to help her outside of class, and she agreed. We continued to meet on a weekly basis. By the end of the term, not only did she have a passing grade, but she understood the material and was helping others.

5. Advice to future student

Never stop learning. The web development industry is constantly moving and changing, and those individuals you can adapt quickly will be the most successful.
