Interactive Media Design

Meet the Professors

Katie Tremblay

tremblayk@algonquincollege.com

Katie Tremblay is a web design and development consultant with experience collaborating on client projects, graphic design and layout, web publishing, video editing, programming, flash animation and project management. Katie is educated in Communications from Ottawa University and is a graduate of Interactive Multimedia at Algonquin. With an ambition to learn and teach others, Katie strives to motivate and educate students on the fast-paced field of Interactive Media Management. She is the co-coordinator & professor for IMD.

Michael Eisenbraun

eisenbm@algonquincollege.com

Michael is currently a professor at Algonquin College in the School of Media and Design. He teaches Client & Server-side Web Development for IMD. He is born and raised in South Dakota, USA, and immigrated to Canada in Aug. 2014. He is married with 3 children. He designed his very first web site in 1997. He graduated in 2005 from Iowa State University. And received first web design job in Nov. 2006. He started teaching in Jan. 2016. He teaches Client & Server-side Web Development.

Hussein Tayrani

tayranh@algonquincollege.com

Hussein is currently a professor at Algonquin College in the School of Media and Design. He teaches Design, Video and Motion Graphics courses for IMD. Helping his students to thrive and become professionals at work are among his main goals. Hussein's drive is powered by a special fuel he identifies as his 'Coffeetivity', the combination of coffee with creativity. When his colleagues see him tackling a project with a cup of coffee in his hand, there is no doubt he is up to something genius. On a regular day, if not teaching or working on Motion Graphics projects, Hussein is watching, playing or reading news about soccer.
